How It Works

Start by describing your agentic AI workflow in plain language. Be specific about tasks, tools, data types, and requirements.

AgentLens searches the web using DuckDuckGo to find up-to-date information about LLM models relevant to your workflow.

Qwen3 (via Ollama) analyzes the search results and produces structured recommendations tailored to your needs.

Review detailed model cards, filter by type, and compare models side-by-side in the comparison table.
